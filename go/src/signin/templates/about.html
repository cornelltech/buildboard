About the Cornell Tech Co-Curriculum


Cornell Tech is founded on a fresh approach to Masters education. We bring academics and the tech community together to develop transformative ideas and talent, and encourage creative vision, technical depth, and entrepreneurial thinking. We envisioned how education could meet tech needs now and in the future, then developed a powerful curriculum to put that into practice. Our education goes beyond theory, giving students skills in working together in multidisciplinary teams, familiarity with markets, access to thought leaders, a portfolio of work, and a truly entrepreneurial mindset.

If you are interested in learning more about a team, contact J McLoughlin.
